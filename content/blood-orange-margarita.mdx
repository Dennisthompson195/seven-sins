## Ingredients 
- 2 ounces Tequila Blanco 
- 1/2 ounce Triple Sec
- 1 ounce Lime Juice, fresh squeezed
- 1/2 ounce <Link href='/product/blood-orange-cocktail-syrup'>Blood Orange Syrup </Link>

- Garnish: <Link href='/product/lime'>Dehydrated Lime SLice</Link>
- Garnish: Kosher Salt (optional)


## Steps

1. Rim a rocks glass with salt and fill with ice
2. place 2 or 3 slices of lime slices between the ice and the sides of the glass
3. Add the Tequila, Triple Sec, lime juice, and blood orange syrup to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
4. Strain into the prepared glass.
